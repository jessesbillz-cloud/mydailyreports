How It Works
Your Forms, Your Workflow
Four steps. That's it. Upload once, report every day.
01
Your Forms, Not Ours
Upload the exact PDF your company requires. AI maps every field automatically. You fill them in, we generate the final PDF.
02
Talk, Don't Type
Dictate your observations while you walk. Your words land on the form in real time. Everything saves automatically.
03
AI Photo Descriptions
Snap a photo, tap the AI button. It writes a professional description — materials, work activity, location. One tap.
04
One-Tap Submit
Hit submit. A clean PDF using your exact template gets generated and emailed to your project team. Numbering, dates, and file naming handled automatically.

Training Center

Set Up in 5 Minutes — Create your account, create a job, upload your template. That's the whole setup.

Your Daily Workflow — Open your job, fill in the day's work, submit. The PDF generates automatically using your exact template.

Voice Input & Photos — Dictate your daily activities using voice-to-text. Photos drop right into the report. Capture notes all day as you work.

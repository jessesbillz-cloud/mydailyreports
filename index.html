How It Works

Your Forms, Not Ours

Upload the exact PDF your employer, client, or company requires. Our AI reads your document and maps every field automatically. Fillable PDFs with form fields work best — we read the field names directly. You fill them in, we handle the formatting and the final PDF.

Talk, Don't Type

Tap any field and dictate your observations while you walk. Your words land on the form in real time. No stopping, no typing, no lost details. Take notes throughout the day and come back later — everything is saved automatically.

AI Photo Descriptions

Snap a photo inside the app and tap the AI button. It analyzes the image and writes a professional description for your report — materials, work activity, location context. One tap, no typing.

Photos Drop Right In

Take a photo or pick from your library. It drops directly into your report, perfectly sized and organized. No manual resizing, no copy-paste, no fighting with Word or email attachments.

All-Day Capture

Keep the app open while you work. Log activities, observations, and issues as they happen so you're not trying to remember what you saw six hours ago. Your working copy saves automatically.

One-Tap Submit

When you're done, hit submit. The app generates a clean, professional PDF — using your exact template — and emails it to your project team. Report numbering, date stamps, and file naming are all handled automatically.

Built for the Way You Actually Work

Your Forms, Not Ours

Upload the exact PDF or Word templates your employer, client, or company requires. Our AI reads your document and maps every field automatically. You fill them in — we handle the rest.

Talk, Don't Type

High-accuracy voice-to-text lets you dictate observations while you walk. Your words appear on the form in real-time. No stopping, no typing, no lost details.

All-Day Capture

Keep the app open while you work. Log activities, counts, and issues as they happen so you're not trying to remember what you saw six hours ago.

Photos Drop Right In

Snap a photo inside the app. It drops directly into your report, perfectly sized. No manual copy-and-paste. No resizing. No fighting with Word.

Scheduling That Lives on Your Phone

Share a calendar link with your GC or subs. They request site visits through your link, you confirm with one tap. Approved requests sync directly to the calendar app already on your phone — Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, whatever you use. You get notifications the same way you always do. No second app to check, no separate inbox.
